## Unit 3 - Adjectives
&nbsp;
- alter, altera, alterum - other (of two)
- ceterus, cetera, ceterum - the other, the remainder
- idem, eadem, idem - the same
- liber, libera, liberum - free
- socius, socia, socium - allied
