## Unit 1 - Adverbs and Conjunctions
&nbsp;
- autem: *however*, *nonetheless*
- et: *and*
- ibi: *there*
- inde: *from there*
- ita: *so*
  - itaque: *and so*, *accordingly*
- item: *likewise*, *also*
- non: *not*
- postea: *later*
- sed: *but*
- tunc: *then*
