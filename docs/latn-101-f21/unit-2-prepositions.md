## Unit 2 - Prepositions and Conjunctions
&nbsp;
- ad + *acc*: *towards*, *against*
- apud + *acc*: *at*, *with*, *by*, *near*
- ex + *abl*: *out of*, *from*
- in + *abl*: *in*
- in + *acc*: *into*
- per + *acc*: *through*
- super + *acc*: *above*, *on top of*
- ac/atque: and, and besides, and even
- nam: for
- neque/nec: not, and not, also not
- sive: or
